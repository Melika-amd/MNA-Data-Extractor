<td align="left" style="vertical-align:top"> <p style=" margin-top:0pt ; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman;font-weight:bold;text-align:left">Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain Officers; Compensation Arrangements of Certain Officers. </p></td></tr></table> <p style="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><span style="font-style:italic">Resignation of Joseph A. Mills as Interim Chief Executive Officer, President and Director </span></p> <p style="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">On January&#160;5, 2025, Mr.&#160;Joseph&#160;A. Mills informed the Board of Directors (the &#8220;Board&#8221;) of Talos Energy Inc. (the &#8220;Company&#8221;) that he was resigning from his position as Interim Chief Executive Officer and President and as a member of the Board, effective immediately. Mr.&#160;Mills has indicated to the Company that his resignation is not the result of any dispute or disagreement with the Company or any member of the Board or senior management team on any matter relating to the Company&#8217;s operations, policies or practices. </p> <p style="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">In conjunction with his departure, Mr.&#160;Mills is not entitled to any additional benefits or compensation under the Company&#8217;s pre-existing compensation plans and applicable policies and related agreements. In connection with his resignation, Mr.&#160;Mills forfeited all unvested restricted stock units which he was granted as a member of the Board. </p> <p style="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><span style="font-style:italic">Office of the Interim Chief Executive Officer; Appointment of Interim <span style="white-space:nowrap">Co-Presidents,</span> Interim Chief Executive Officer and Principal Executive Officer </span></p> <p style="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">In connection with and following Mr.&#160;Mills&#8217; resignation, effective as of January&#160;6, 2025 (the &#8220;Effective Date&#8221;), the Board created an Office of the Interim Chief Executive Officer (the &#8220;Office of the Interim CEO&#8221;) and appointed the following three senior executives to serve, in addition to their existing roles, as interim <span style="white-space:nowrap">Co-Presidents</span> and as members of the Office of the Interim CEO: (i)&#160;Mr.&#160;William&#160;S.
