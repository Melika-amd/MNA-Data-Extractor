<td align="left" style="vertical-align:top"> <p style=" margin-top:0pt ; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman;font-weight:bold;text-align:left">Submission of Matters to a Vote of Security Holders. </p></td></tr></table> <p style="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">CONSOL Energy Inc., a Delaware corporation (&#8220;CONSOL&#8221;), held a special meeting of stockholders (the &#8220;Special Meeting&#8221;) on January&#160;9, 2025 for CONSOL&#8217;s stockholders to vote on the matters described below in connection with the merger of equals transaction contemplated by the Agreement and Plan of Merger, dated as of August&#160;20, 2024 (the &#8220;Merger Agreement&#8221;), by and among CONSOL, Mountain Range Merger Sub Inc., a Delaware corporation and a wholly owned subsidiary of CONSOL (&#8220;Merger Sub&#8221;), and Arch Resources, Inc., a Delaware corporation (&#8220;Arch&#8221;). Upon the terms and subject to the conditions of the Merger Agreement, Merger Sub will merge with and into Arch (the &#8220;Merger&#8221;), with Arch continuing as the surviving corporation in the Merger and a wholly owned subsidiary of CONSOL. </p> <p style="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">A total of 23,814,270 shares of common stock, par value $0.01 per share, of CONSOL (&#8220;CONSOL Common Stock&#8221;), out of a total of 29,394,466 shares of CONSOL Common Stock outstanding and entitled to vote as of the close of business on November&#160;26, 2024, the record date for the Special Meeting, were present via the Special Meeting website or represented by proxy, constituting a quorum with respect to a vote on the CONSOL Issuance Proposal (as defined below) and the CONSOL Charter Amendment Proposal (as defined below). </p> <p style="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Set forth below is a summary of the final voting results on each matter submitted to a vote of stockholders at the Special Meeting.
