<Center><DIV STYLE="width:8.5in" align="left">
 <P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="right"><B>Exhibit 99.1 </B></P> <P STYLE="font-size:12pt;margin-top:0pt;margin-bottom:0pt">&nbsp;</P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:0pt;margin-bottom:0pt">


<IMG SRC="g852697g0110060543249.jpg" ALT="LOGO">
 </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B><FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">Li-Cycle</FONT> Provides Preliminary Commercial and Operational
Highlights for 2024 </B></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><I>Company&#146;s largest source of battery feedstock during 2024 was a U.S.-headquartered, vertically integrated
EV and battery manufacturer with a substantial global electric vehicle (&#147;EV&#148;) market share </I></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><I>Continued focus on securing a
full financing package for the Rochester Hub and optimizing Spoke performance </I></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B>TORONTO, Ontario (January 10, 2025)</B> &#150; <U><FONT
STYLE="white-space:nowrap">Li-Cycle</FONT> Holdings Corp.</U> (NYSE: LICY) <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">(&#147;Li-Cycle&#148;</FONT> or the &#147;Company&#148;), a leading global <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">lithium-ion</FONT> battery
resource recovery company, is pleased to report preliminary commercial and operational highlights for full-year 2024. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:18pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B>Commercial Highlights </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">Li-Cycle</FONT> continued to gain commercial traction in 2024, bolstering its position as a preferred <FONT
STYLE="white-space:nowrap">lithium-ion</FONT> battery recycling partner. Its largest customers comprise leading global battery supply chain companies, including EV OEMs, battery manufacturers, miners, and raw material buyers. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">In 2024, the largest source of the Company&#146;s battery feedstock was a U.S.-headquartered, vertically integrated EV and battery manufacturer with a
substantial global EV market share. Throughout 2024, <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">Li-Cycle</FONT> supported approximately 13 prominent EV manufacturers and approximately 15 key battery cell and material producers with the Company&#146;s
sustainable recycling services. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">&#147;We are pleased to be able to widen our commercial footprint for battery feedstock for our Spoke network in the
United States and Germany. Our top source of battery feedstock during the past year was a leading U.S. EV and battery manufacturer and is indicative of our position as a preferred recycling partner for the largest blue-chip companies in the global
battery supply chain,&#148; said Ajay Kochhar, <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">Li-Cycle</FONT> President and CEO. &#147;Our sustainable, battery-agnostic recycling technology &#151; which can process full-pack batteries regardless of form factor
and without the need to dismantle or discharge &#151; continues to provide value for our global customers.&#148; </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:18pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B>Rochester Hub and Spoke Operations
Highlights </B></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">Li-Cycle</FONT> is continuing to prioritize securing a full financing package to support the construction
restart of the Rochester Hub project and to satisfy requirements for first advance under the $475&nbsp;million loan facility from the U.S. Department of Energy (DOE). </P>
</DIV></Center>

<Center><DIV STYLE="width:8.5in" align="left">

 <P STYLE="margin-top:0pt;margin-bottom:0pt">


<IMG SRC="g852697g0110060543249.jpg" ALT="LOGO">
 </P> <p STYLE="margin-top:0pt;margin-bottom:0pt ; font-size:8pt">&nbsp;</P>
 <P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">Li-Cycle</FONT> produced approximately 5,370 tonnes of black mass and
equivalents<SUP STYLE="font-size:75%; vertical-align:top">1</SUP> (&#147;BM&amp;E&#148;) in full-year 2024, and approximately 1,200 tonnes of BM&amp;E in Q4 2024. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The Company is continuing to focus on its Spoke optimization initiatives to establish a financially accretive and self-sufficient Spoke business. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">&#147;Our two main goals for the Company are clear: we are focused on securing a full financing package to underpin the restart of construction of the
Rochester Hub and starting to draw on the DOE loan, alongside implementing initiatives to enhance Spoke performance and improve cash flow to establish a self-sufficient Spoke business.&#148; </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:18pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B>Balance Sheet Position </B></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">As of December&nbsp;31, 2024, <FONT
STYLE="white-space:nowrap">Li-Cycle</FONT> had cash and cash equivalents of $22.6&nbsp;million, compared to $32.2&nbsp;million as of September&nbsp;30, 2024. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Between August&nbsp;12, 2024, and December&nbsp;31, 2024, <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">Li-Cycle</FONT> raised $16.3&nbsp;million of net proceeds by
issuing an aggregate of 7,403,621 of the Company&#146;s common shares under its <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap"><FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">at-the-market</FONT></FONT> program at a weighted average price of $2.25 per share. The number of
issued and outstanding common shares as of December&nbsp;31, 2024, was 30,427,796. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The Company expects to release its full-year 2024 financial and
operational results in the first half of March 2025. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:18pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B>About <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">Li-Cycle</FONT> Holdings Corp. </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">Li-Cycle</FONT> (NYSE: LICY) is a leading global <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">lithium-ion</FONT> battery resource recovery
company. Established in 2016, and with major customers and partners around the world, <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">Li-Cycle&#146;s</FONT> mission is to recover critical battery-grade materials to create a domestic closed-loop battery supply
chain for a clean energy future. The Company leverages its innovative, sustainable and patent-protected Spoke&nbsp;&amp; Hub Technologies<SUP STYLE="font-size:75%; vertical-align:top">&#153;</SUP> to recycle all different types of <FONT
STYLE="white-space:nowrap">lithium-ion</FONT> batteries. At our Spokes, or <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">pre-processing</FONT> facilities, we recycle battery manufacturing scrap and
<FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap"><FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">end-of-life</FONT></FONT> batteries to produce black mass, a powder-like substance which contains a number of valuable metals, including lithium, nickel and cobalt. At our future
Hubs, or post-processing facilities, we plan to process black mass to produce critical battery-grade materials, including lithium carbonate, for the <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">lithium-ion</FONT> battery supply chain. For more information,
<U>visit <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">https://li-cycle.com/</FONT></U>. </P> <P STYLE="line-height:8.0pt;margin-top:0pt;margin-bottom:2pt;border-bottom:1px solid #000000;width:11%">&nbsp;</P>
