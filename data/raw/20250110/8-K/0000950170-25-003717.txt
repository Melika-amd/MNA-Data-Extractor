The financial results included in this release pertaining to the fourth quarter and fiscal 2024 are preliminary, unaudited, and subject to final review and adjustment.</font><font style="color:#000000;white-space:pre-wrap;font-size:12pt;font-family:Times New Roman;min-width:fit-content;"> </font></p>
