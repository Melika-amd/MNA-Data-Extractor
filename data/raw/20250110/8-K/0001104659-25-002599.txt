<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify; color: #212529">Effective January 10, 2025, the
holders of a majority of the voting power of the capital stock of Mustang Bio, Inc. (the &#8220;Company&#8221;) executed a written consent
authorizing, for purposes of complying with Nasdaq Listing Rule 5635(d), the issuance of up to an aggregate of 34,767,934 shares of the
Company&#8217;s common stock underlining certain outstanding warrants issued by the Company pursuant to (A) that certain previously disclosed
Investor Inducement Letter Agreement, dated as of October 24, 2024 (the &#8220;Inducement Letter&#8221;), with a certain institutional
investor (the &#8220;Investor&#8221;), and (B) that certain previously disclosed engagement letter, dated as of October 23, 2024 (the
&#8220;Engagement Letter&#8217;), with H.C. Wainwright &amp; Co., LLC (the &#8220;Placement Agent&#8221;), in an amount equal to or in
excess of 20% of the number of shares of common stock outstanding immediately prior to the issuance of such warrants (collectively, the
&#8220;Warrant Share Issuance&#8221;).</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify; color: #212529">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify; color: #212529">The written consent was signed
by the holders of 6,552,781 shares of the Company&#8217;s issued and outstanding common stock and 250,000 shares of the Company&#8217;s
issued and outstanding Class&#8239;A Preferred Stock. Each share of common stock and Class&#8239;A Common Stock entitles the holder thereof
to one vote on all matters submitted to stockholders and each share of Class A Preferred Stock has the voting power of 1.1 times (A) the
number of outstanding shares of common stock plus (B) the whole shares of common stock into which the outstanding shares of Class&#8239;A
Common Stock and Class&#8239;A Preferred Stock are convertible, divided by the number of outstanding shares of Class A Preferred Stock,
or 285 votes per share as of January 10, 2025. Accordingly, the holders of approximately 57% of the voting power of the Company&#8217;s
