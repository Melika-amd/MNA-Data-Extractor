    <div style="text-align: left; text-indent: 36pt; font-family: 'Times New Roman', Times, serif; font-size: 12pt;">On December 26, 2024, Orange County Bancorp, Inc. (the &#8220;Company&#8221;) announced a two-for-one forward stock split (the &#8220;Stock Split&#8221;) of the
      Company&#8217;s issued common stock to be effected through the filing of an amendment to the Company's Certificate of Incorporation, as amended (the &#8220;Amendment&#8221;) with the Secretary of the State of Delaware. The Company filed the Amendment to effect the
      Stock Split and proportionately increase the number of shares of the Company&#8217;s authorized common stock from 15 million to 30 million. The Amendment, which became effective at 4:01 p.m. Eastern Time on January 10, 2025, is filed as Exhibit 3.1 to this
      Current Report on Form 8-K.</div>

    </div>
