<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: justify"><b>Item 5.07. Submission of Matters to a Vote of Security

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: justify">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: justify">On January 9, 2025, certain stockholders (the &#8220;Majority
Stockholders&#8221;) of Ethema Health Corporation (the &#8220;Company&#8221;) took action by written consent (&#8220;Written Consent&#8221;).
