 <p style="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman;font-weight:bold"><span style="text-decoration:underline">Item&#8201;2.03 Creation of a Direct Financial Obligation or an Obligation under an Off-Balance Sheet Arrangement of Registrant. </span></p> <p style="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">In connection with the merger described in Item 8.01 below, and pursuant to supplemental indentures dated January&#160;10, 2025, United Bankshares, Inc. (&#8220;United&#8221;) assumed all of the obligations of Piedmont Bancorp, Inc. (&#8220;Piedmont&#8221;) with respect to its outstanding subordinated notes consisting of: (i)&#160;$17,000,000 of fixed-to-floating rate subordinated notes due September&#160;1, 2030 with respect to which UMB Bank, N.A. serves as Trustee; and (ii)&#160;$3,000,000 of fixed-to-floating rate subordinated notes due September&#160;1, 2030, with respect to which UMB Bank, N.A. serves as Trustee. </p> <p style="margin-top:18pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman;font-weight:bold"><span style="text-decoration:underline">Item&#8201;8.01 Other Events. </span></p> <p style="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Effective as of 5:01 p.m. on January&#160;10, 2025 (the &#8220;Effective Time&#8221;), United completed its previously announced acquisition of Piedmont. Piedmont was merged with and into United (the &#8220;Merger&#8221;), pursuant to the terms of the Agreement and Plan of Merger, dated May&#160;9, 2024, by and between United and Piedmont (the &#8220;Agreement&#8221;). The Merger was accounted for under the acquisition method of accounting. </p> <p style="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Immediately following the Merger, The Piedmont Bank, a wholly-owned subsidiary of Piedmont, merged with and into United Bank, a wholly-owned subsidiary of United (the &#8220;Bank Merger&#8221;) pursuant to the Bank Agreement and Plan of Merger dated May&#160;9, 2024 (the &#8220;Bank Plan of Merger&#8221;).
