<td style="width:11%;vertical-align:top" align="left"><span style="font-weight:bold">Item&#8201;3.01.</span></td>
<td align="left" style="vertical-align:top"> <p style=" margin-top:0pt ; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman;font-weight:bold;text-align:left">Notice of Delisting or Failure to Satisfy a Continued Listing Rule or Standard; Transfer of Listing. </p></td></tr></table> <p style="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">On December&#160;17, 2024, Gencor Industries, Inc. (the &#8220;Company&#8221;) filed a Notification of Late Filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission (the &#8220;SEC&#8221;) on Form <span style="white-space:nowrap">12b-25</span> indicating that the filing of its Annual Report on Form <span style="white-space:nowrap">10-K</span> for the year ended September&#160;30, 2024 (the &#8220;Form <span style="white-space:nowrap">10-K&#8221;)</span> would be delayed and the reasons for the delay. On January&#160;3, 2025, the Company received a notice (the &#8220;Delinquency Notification&#8221;) from NYSE Regulation (the &#8220;NYSE&#8221;) indicating the Company is not in compliance with the NYSE American LLC (&#8220;NYSE American&#8221;) continued listing standards as a result of its failure to timely file its Form <span style="white-space:nowrap">10-K</span> with the SEC prior to December&#160;31, 2024, the end of the extension period provided by the Form <span style="white-space:nowrap">12b-25,</span> and is now subject to the procedures set forth in Section&#160;1007 of the NYSE American Company Guide. </p> <p style="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The NYSE informed the Company that, under the rules of the NYSE American, the Company has six months from the Form <span style="white-space:nowrap">10-K</span> filing due date of December&#160;31, 2024, to regain compliance with the NYSE American listing standards by filing the Form <span style="white-space:nowrap">10-K</span> with the SEC.
