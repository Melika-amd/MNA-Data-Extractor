<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">Arch Resources,&#160;Inc.,
a Delaware corporation (the &#8220;Company&#8221;), held a special meeting of stockholders (the &#8220;Special Meeting&#8221;) on January&#160;9,
2025 for the Company&#8217;s stockholders to vote on the matters described below in connection with the merger of equals transaction contemplated
by the Agreement and Plan of Merger, dated as of August&#160;20, 2024 (the &#8220;Merger Agreement&#8221;), by and among the Company,
CONSOL Energy Inc. (&#8220;CONSOL&#8221;), and Mountain Range Merger Sub Inc., a Delaware corporation and a wholly owned subsidiary of
CONSOL (&#8220;Merger Sub&#8221;). Upon the terms and subject to the conditions of the Merger Agreement, Merger Sub will merge with and
into the Company (the &#8220;Merger&#8221;), with the Company continuing as the surviving corporation in the Merger and a wholly owned
subsidiary of CONSOL.</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">The following proposals were
submitted by the Board of Directors of the Company to a vote of stockholders at the Special Meeting, and the final results of the voting
on each proposal is noted below. On the record date of November&#160;26, 2024, there were 18,108,609 shares of the Company&#8217;s common
stock outstanding and entitled to vote. Each proposal is described in more detail in the definitive joint proxy statement/prospectus filed
with the Securities and Exchange Commission on November&#160;26, 2024. The Merger is expected to be completed on or around January&#160;14,
