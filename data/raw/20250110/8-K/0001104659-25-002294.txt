Industry and Security (&#8220;BIS&#8221;) released new interim final rules adding new companies to the U.S. Entity List and further regulating
the export of certain semiconductor capital equipment products and services to China-based customers (the &#8220;2024 BIS Rules&#8221;).&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">Based on a preliminary review of the 2024 BIS Rules, we currently estimate
the associated impact to be a reduction in total annual revenue in the range of $20 million to $50 million in Fiscal Year 2025.&#160;&#160;
This estimate is subject to change, as we continue to assess the impact of these new rules.</p>
<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&#160;</p>
